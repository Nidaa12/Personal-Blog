Welcome to the little world of sweets, full of delicious flavors!

Dessert dishes are not a new thing. 200 years ago, people considered these dishes as a way to celebrate. Honey was known as the oldest sweet that people ate in ancient times. Years passed and the techniques for making sweets diversified. Whereas they used to make sweets by combining fruits and nuts with honey, today there are countless sweet dishes.

You should follow these tips so that you can prepare sweets at home in a delicious and beautiful way:

- You must read the recipe carefully and carefully before starting to prepare the sweets.
- Prepare all the ingredients in the same quantity mentioned.
- ⁠ Don’t be afraid to experiment and take risks. Add your own touches to make them stand out.
- ⁠ Pay attention to the method of presentation and decoration. Don’t forget that the eye also eats.

Cheesecake is one of the delicious recipes loved by most people, as it is one of the types of cakes. The origin of this meal goes back to Greece. There is no single recipe for preparing cheesecake, as you can prepare it with your favorite flavor.

There are many types of cheesecake:
- Vanilla cheesecake.
- ⁠ Chocolate cheesecake.
- ⁠ Fruits cheesecake(berries, lemon, mango).
- ⁠ Lotus heesecake.
